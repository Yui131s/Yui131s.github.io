Group Project 1 (Moodboard)

01/09/2020

The first class after the mid-trimester break, we started a group project. We discussed what kinds of website we want to work on, we decided to work on a portfolio website for a design company. We split ourselves into design team and coding team. I was assigned into a design team. After the class, I started designing a mood board. The theme we decided was simple and monochrome. I thought using green would be cool, so I put some images of plants and color palette of green.
